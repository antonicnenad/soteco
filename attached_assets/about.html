Ko smo mi?

Who we are?

SOTECO D.O.O. je kompanija sa preko 25 godina iskustva u proizvodnji i ugradnji ventilatora i opreme za ventilacione sisteme. Naša osnovna delatnost je proizvodnja industrijskih ventilatora i opreme za ventilacione sisteme.

SOTECO D.O.O. is a company with more than 25 years of experience in manufacturing and installation of fans and equipment for ventilation systems. Our core business is the production of industrial fans and equipment for ventilation systems.

Kompanija SOTECO D.O.O. je osnovana 1997. godine u Subotici. Od tada pa do danas, kompanija je kontinuirano razvijala svoje poslovanje i proširivala asortiman proizvoda.

SOTECO D.O.O. was founded in 1997 in Belgrade. Since then until today, the company has continuously developed its business and expanded its product range.

Danas SOTECO D.O.O. posluje na tržištu Srbije i regiona, a proizvodi se izvoze u više od 20 zemalja širom sveta. Naš tim čine visoko kvalifikovani stručnjaci sa dugogodišnjim iskustvom.

Today, SOTECO D.O.O. operates in the market of Serbia and the region, and products are exported to more than 20 countries worldwide. Our team consists of highly qualified professionals with many years of experience.

Naša misija

Our mission

Naša misija je da kroz kontinuirani razvoj i unapređenje kvaliteta proizvoda i usluga postanemo lider u oblasti ventilacionih sistema u regionu, zadržavajući pri tome povoljan odnos cene i kvaliteta.

Our mission is to become a leader in the field of ventilation systems in the region through continuous development and improvement of product and service quality, while maintaining a favorable price-quality ratio.

Naša vizija

Our vision

Vizija kompanije SOTECO D.O.O. je da bude prepoznatljiv brend u oblasti proizvodnje ventilatora i ventilacione opreme, sa jakim prisustvom na domaćem i inostranom tržištu.

The vision of SOTECO D.O.O. is to be a recognizable brand in the field of fan and ventilation equipment production, with a strong presence in the domestic and foreign market.
