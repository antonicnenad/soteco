Who are Soteco partners?

Farmaceutska Industrija

Proizvođači automatskih mašina za obradu i pakovanje farmaceutskih proizvoda poput; tableta, kapsula, sirupa i slično.

Pharmaceutical Industry

Manufacturers of automatic machines for processing and packaging pharmaceutical products such as tablets, capsules, syrups and similar.

Prehrambena Industrija

Proizvođači opreme za prehrambenu industriju. Mašine za preradu i pakovanje mesa, voća, povrća, sira, čokolade i slično.

Food Industry

Manufacturers of equipment for the food industry. Machines for processing and packaging meat, fruits, vegetables, cheese, chocolate and similar.

Industrija pakovanja

Proizvođači materijala za pakovanje čajeva, bilja, kafe, konfekcije, kozmetike i higijenskih proizvoda.

Packaging Industry

Manufacturers of packaging materials for teas, herbs, coffee, confectionery, cosmetics and hygiene products.

Nekoliko reči o nama

Realizovani projekti su naša najbolja preporuka. Porodična firma Soteco doo, postoji već 25 godina na našim prostorima. Za dve i po decenije postojanja uspeli smo da zauzmemo lidersku poziciju, kako na našem tako i na tržištu regiona.

A few words about us

Completed projects are our best recommendation. The family company Soteco doo has existed for 25 years in our area. In two and a half decades of existence, we have managed to take a leading position, both in our country and in the regional market.
